OUR STORY

Ours is a journey that began with the warmth of friendship, evolved with shared laughter, and grew into something truly beautiful. After six years filled with unforgettable memories, adventures, and milestones, we’re finally ready to take the next big step.

OUR FIRST MEETING

We first met at college, where she was my junior. I had a secret crush on her long before we even spoke. One day, we finally struck up a conversation, and the rest, as they say, is history. From that moment, I knew there was something special about her.

OUR FIRST DATE

Our first date was a movie night, watching *Stree*, a quirky horror-comedy that had us both laughing and occasionally hiding behind our popcorn. It was the perfect start – a mix of laughter, a little fear, and an unspoken excitement of being together.

THE PROPOSAL

There was no grand, formal proposal – it was something that grew naturally, a shared understanding. By 2022, we both knew we wanted to spend our lives together, and soon, our families became part of the dream. Our engagement was more a celebration of our journey than just an event.

THE ENGAGEMENT

On a leap day, February 29, 2024, we exchanged rings, symbolizing our shared promises. It was the beginning of a new chapter in our story, marking the transition from “you and me” to “us.” Surrounded by family and friends, it felt like the perfect moment.
